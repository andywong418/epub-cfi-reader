Preface

This book is concerned with polar organometallic reagents and their use in organic synthesis. It will be useful in undergraduate chemistry courses and also to the practising chemist looking for a concise overview of this area. My aim is to provide sufficient information for the student to be able to propose polar organometallic reagents to solve synthetic problems, and also to avoid the pitfalls involved in their use.
