Selected ionic radii

Ionic radii are given (in picometres, pm) for the most common oxidation states and coordination geometries. The coordination number is given in parentheses. All d-block species are low-spin unless labelled with †, in which case values for high-spin are quoted. Most data are taken from R.D. Shannon, Acta Cryst., 1976, A32, 751, where values for other coordination geometries can be found. Where Shannon values are not available, Pauling ionic radii are quoted and are indicated by *.
