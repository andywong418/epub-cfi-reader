Problems

The first section contains problems concerning structure and bonding and the answers lie within the text, or are closely related to discussions in the text. Problems in the next section are taken from real experimental situations; the answers can be found by consulting the reference cited but a word of encouragement: there is not always a unique solution to the spectroscopic data and if your answer does not match that in the cited paper, consider it carefully. It may be an alternative solution consistent with the data given in the question. What other experimental data are needed to confirm your (or the literature) structures?
