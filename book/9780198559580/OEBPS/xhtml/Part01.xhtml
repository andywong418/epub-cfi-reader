I Fundamentals: what is catalysis?

Catalysis is one of the most important technologies in our modern world. We depend on it to produce materials, such as plastics, from oil; we depend on it to produce fuel to power our cars; we depend on it to remove the pollutants emitted from the engines of those cars; we even depend on it for the functioning and growth of our own bodies. It is therefore very important that we ask ourselves the question, ‘what is catalysis?’ In this section the fundamental basis of catalysis will be described, focusing largely on the crucial part of a catalyst, namely the surface layer, where all the chemical action takes place.
