Founding Editor’s Foreword

Foundations of physical chemistry: worked examples is the companion Primer tothe internationally highly acclaimed Foundations of physical chemistry (OCP 40).Together these Primers are designed to facilitate the transition from chemistrystudies at School to those at University by providing a concise and elementarycoverage of core topics both descriptively and by way of worked examples.

Foundations of physical chemistry: worked examples has been extremely wellconstructed in a very user friendly format by a master chemist and two of hisnewly qualified apprentices. Students who aspire to take up apprenticeship inchemistry as well as their teachers will find this Primer invaluable.

Stephen G. Davies

The Dyson Perrins Laboratory, University of Oxford
